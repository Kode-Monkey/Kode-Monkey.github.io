Back in 1973 the Rehabilitation Act was put in place for people with disabilities.
In 1993 & 1998 it was amended with Section 508 & 504 to include electronic documents and software applications.

In 2010 the DOJ put out Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) making it more an unwritten requirement that all online documents be accessible to all users no matter their disability.

We follow the WCAG Principles call POUR:

  • Perceivable
  • Operable
  • Understandable
  • Robust

Basicly what they are telling us is that any information about your business that you offer online to the general the public is considered to be public domain and therefore must be accessible to anyone with any disability.
