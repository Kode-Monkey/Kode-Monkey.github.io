Web Accessibile is not an Option


You may be thinking, "Who Governs Web Accessibility?", "What does it matter to my business?", "I will worry about when the time comes".

You are right. At the moment it is not a requirement, nor is it mandated, however, the DOJ is working to change this and you don't want to be the one cited for none compliance if they do mandate it.

You are risking lawsuits and a variety of legal compliance issues if you are not meeting accessibility standards. If you are a gov site you are even more at risk.

In 2016, over 240 lawsuits, were filed against companies for their websites not being accessible to the blind and visually impaired.

Aside from ADA compliance other reasons to make sure your website is accessible is:

  • Overall functionality
  • Improve search engine optimization (SEO)
  • Make future maintenance and updates easier
  • Create wider device support

Web Accessibility help you reach more of the customers who are looking for your products or services.
